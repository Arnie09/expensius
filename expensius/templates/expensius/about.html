What is Expensius?


Expensius is monetary control platform that helps you track your monney and how you spend the money. Currently its a product in development! Here are how you are going to work with this tool:

  • Currently you have given your account a name and entered a starting amount that your acccount has.
  • Next you can add your daily transactions and monitor them.
  • You can delete, or modify any transactions in your account... remember that if you modify/change a transaction of the past it will affect all fututre transactions
  • At no time, can the money in your account be negetive.
  • You cannot enter a future transaction into the system
